Mu butumwa Bwiza

Yesu ashimwe benedata muri kristo Yesu mbanje kubasuhuza Imana y'amahoro ibampere umugisha. Uru rubuga rwitwa ubutumwa bwiza. None reka twibaze iki kibazo! Ubutumwa bwiza Niki? Turifadhisha Bibiliya yera mu gitabo cy'ubutumwa bwiza uko bwanditswe na Mariko 16:15 havuga ngo; "ababwira ati: mujye mu bihugu byose, mwigishe abaremwe bose ubutumwa bwiza. Uwizera akabatizwa azakizwa, ariko utizera azacirwaho iteka." Ubwo butumwa bwiza yahaye abigishwa se bwo Ni ubuhe? Igisubizo turagisanga muri Luka 24:47 havuga ngo : "Kandi ko kwihana no kubabarirwa ibyaha bikwiye kubwirwa amahanga yose mu izina rye, bahereye i Yerusalemu." Uru rubuga rero tuzajya dusangiriraho ijambo ry'Iamana nkifunguro ryacu rya buri nk'abakristo bategereje Kandi biteguye gusanganira umwami wacu Yesu kristo ujyiye kuza gutwara abo yacunguye mu myaka 2000 ishize yose. Ndabasaba Kandi mbinginga ngo murarike n'abandi mubatumira(inviting) kugira ngo twese tutazacika aya mahirwe Imana yo mu ijuru yaduteganyirije. Kandi ntabwo ubutumwa bwiza bukwiye kudukoza isoni, ahubwo bukwiye kudutera imbaraga no gukomerera mu mwami. Imana ibane namwe mwese mu izina ry'umucunguzi w'ijuru n'isi Yesu kristo, Amena. Njye nitwa Robert MBARUSHIMANA uwamagawe na Kristo ngo namamaze ishimwe ry'iyaduhamagaye ikadukura muri egiputa y'ibyaha ikaducungura amaraso y'igiciro cyinshi.
